Why MPD

  • PNG OVERSEAS utilizes Mud Pressure Drilling technology as opposed to the more traditional approaches in order to manage risks that come with well drilling. Application of the MPD-UBD technology enables to essentially mitigate risks of well control loss in the course of drilling and the related therewith secondary risks. At the same time, the said technologies are cheaper than traditional solutions and techniques applied, making it a more attractive feature to the Client.
  • The use of this technology is recommended when drilling in the so-called "narrow window zones". In such conditions, insignificant variations of mud density may cause either mud loss or oil/gas/water kicks.
  • MPD is also used in situations when it is difficult to forecast the presence and/or characteristics of narrow drilling window zones because of insufficient geological data of drilling concession area.
